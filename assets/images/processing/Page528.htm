This view of the frame in the extractor shows the interior of the extractor.  All parts of the extractor are constructed of stainless steel.  A Plexiglas cover tops the extractor.  This type of extractor extracts the honey from both sides of the comb while the reel holding the comb spins inside the extractor.  Honey flows out of the comb by centrifugal force and lands on the stainless steel wall of the extractor.  It then runs down the wall and out the spout in the bottom of the extractor into a collection tank.  This leaves the frame of honeycomb still intact and ready for the bees to fill it again next season.
