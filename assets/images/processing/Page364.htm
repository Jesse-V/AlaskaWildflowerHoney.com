Honey is pumped from the collection tank into the settling tank.  This tank holds about 1500 pounds of honey. Small suspended particles of wax that have escaped the baffling of the collection tank rise to the surface of the settling tank.  Honey is allowed to settle here from several days to several weeks.  The tank is made of stainless steel and has no heater hooked to it so it stays at room temperature.  A plexiglass cover allows us to monitor the level of honey as well as providing a method of access for skimming the small particles of wax off the surface of the honey.  It also provides access for cleaning the tank.
