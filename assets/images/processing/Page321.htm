I don't know for sure what this thing is called, we call it the merry-go-round because that is what the fellow called it who sold it to us.  We do know that it plays a great role in the extracting process.  Frames from the uncapper are placed here while waiting for the extractor to finish the batch that is running.  It also allows the frames of honeycomb to be rotated toward the uncapping table for a final check before placing them into the extractor.
